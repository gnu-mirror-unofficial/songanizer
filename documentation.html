GNU Songanizer - Documentation

Documentation

Songanizer documentation can be found at http://www.gnu.org/software/songanizer/manual/. You may also find more information about Songanizer by looking at /usr/doc/songanizer/ or /usr/local/doc/songanizer/ or by looking at man pages (man songanizer at the shell prompt) on your system.
